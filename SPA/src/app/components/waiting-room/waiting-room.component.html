Privacy Policy for EletricGo

At EletricGo, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and share personal data when you use our web application, EletricGo.

Personal Data We Collect

We collect the following personal data from you when you use EletricGo:

  • First and last name: We collect your first and last name to show in your profile and to identify you within the application.
  • Email address and password: We collect your email address and password for the purpose of logging you into EletricGo.
  • Phone number: We collect your phone number in order to contact you via SMS when necessary.

We do not collect any other personal data from you or share your personal data with any third parties.

Use of Personal Data

We use your personal data to provide you with the services offered by EletricGo, including but not limited to:

  • Personalizing your experience within the application
  • Communicating with you about your account or the services we offer
  • Improving the functionality and user experience of EletricGo

Your Rights

If you wish to access, rectify, erase, restrict, or object to the processing of your personal data, or if you wish to exercise your right to data portability, you may contact the EletricGo administrator to request these actions. Please note that the administrator may request proof of identity before fulfilling your request.

You may also request that your personal data be anonymized, in which case we will no longer be able to provide you with personalized recommendations or communication. If you wish to anonymize your personal data, you can do it by clicking in your profile picture and than the option "Cancel account".

You also have the right to file a complaint with a supervisory authority if you believe that we have not complied with the requirements of the GDPR with regard to your personal data.

Updates to this Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We encourage you to review this privacy policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting the personal data we collect.

Contact Us
